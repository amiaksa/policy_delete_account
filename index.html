Account Deletion

This page explains how to delete your {{APP_NAME}} account from inside the app and what happens to your data.

Delete your account inside the app

  1. Open {{APP_NAME}} and go to Settings (or Account).
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your choice.

Deletion happens immediately. If you’re asked to sign in again, it’s for security (recent login required).

What data is deleted

  • Your authentication record (email/username) stored in Firebase Authentication.
  • Any app data tied only to your authentication record (if applicable).

No confirmation email is sent, but the app shows a confirmation state after deletion.

What may remain temporarily

System logs or encrypted backups maintained by our infrastructure providers may persist for a limited period for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. These are automatically purged on a rolling schedule (typically within 90 days) and are not used to recreate your account.

Data safety & encryption

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • If your child uses the app, we follow Google Play Families Policy requirements. No advertising IDs are used for children.

Delete data without deleting your account

If the app includes options like “Clear history” or “Reset progress,” you can use them without deleting the entire account. Otherwise, contact us and we’ll help.

Contact

If you need help with account deletion, please email support@amiaksa.com from the address associated with your account so we can verify ownership.
